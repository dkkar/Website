Stabilized Footage

Posted on March 12, 2020 by Mauris Winata

    We were able to test the gimbal for its camera stabilization abilty, for the pitch axis. The captured footage without any active stabilization can be seen below (right). The swaying of the gimbal and the lack of correction by the motors is captured as well (video on left).

    With active stabilization, the capture footage is much smoother. This can be seen in the video on the right. The video on the left shows the motor action. The camera carriage can be seen to remain vertical despite the constant swaying.
