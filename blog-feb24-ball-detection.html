Detecting Basketball

Posted on February 24, 2020 by Sanjay Srinivasan

    The below image is the first detection performed on the Jetson Nano. Steps were taken to convert the trained model into an optimized version that can be run on the Jetson. We are currently investigating ways to further optimize the operations of the network so that the inference is as fast as it can be on the Jetson Nano The bounding boxes showcase the location of the image predicted by the model along with the confidence containing a probability of that image being a basketball.
