The Problem

With the advent of smartphones and handheld video recording technology, filming sporting events has become increasingly popular. But due to the innate fast nature of sports such as basketball, camera operators struggle with filming fast moving objects accurately and with stability.

Features

Bold and Brash will build on top of the traditional camera gimbal feature set, namely, video stabilization, while including the capability for basketball tracking.

How It Works

Our gimbal works by using a permanently mounted tracking camera to detect a basketball. All detection related processing is performed by an Nvidia Jetson. The Jetson is not only used to locate the ball, but also predict where the ball will be. This information is then sent to an Arduino, which controls the motor-controllers. There are two motor-controllers; one shared between the pitch and roll axes, and a second for the yaw axis. The gimbal-controllers then control the gimbal motors such that the tracking camera sees the ball. Any camera of your choice can be placed on the carriage. As the tracking camera is also attached to the carriage, it is ensured that your personal camera also follows the ball.
