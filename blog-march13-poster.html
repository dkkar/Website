Poster

Posted on March 13 2020 by Kalen Saldanha

    A poster designed for the Symposium was created to give viewers a short and descriptive insight of Bold & Brash. The left side describes the goals, motivation and objective that were used to develop the project solution during the initial design phase. The right side describes the key elements of the electric, mechanical and data capture design that were used in developing Bold & Brash. The final developed design is shown descriptively as well as visually in the middle of the poster. The “How it Works” section visually describes how Bold & Brash tracks and responds to basketball movement.
