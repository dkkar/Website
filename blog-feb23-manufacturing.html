Completed Manufacturing

Posted on February 23, 2020 by Kalen Saldanha

    Completed manufacturing of gimbal motor structure and integrated it with motors, 3D brackets and plugs, and acrylic fixtures. Various PVC parts were cut and drilled according to the CAD model using band saws and drill presses. Size adjustments were made based on discrepancies in actual models and fittings. The gimbal was then disassembled for spray painting. PVC pipes for the stand along with the fixtures were also cut and spray painted.
