Sway Fix

Posted on March 09, 2020 by Kalen Saldanha

The Problem

    A slight modification to the gimbal stand has been made to ensure that the gimbal hangs more ‘upright’ as it was initially intended. Due to its weight, the bottom of the gimbal would swing forward on the stand as shown (left).

The Fix:

This issue causes issues with the yaw axis movement, as the gimbal now has to overcome the force of gravity in order to rotate in this axis. Unfortunately, the yaw axis motor is not strong enough to overcome this force, so this issue needed to be addressed. To fix this, holes were drilled into the gimbal handle frame and the ‘forks’ of the stand as shown (left).

As such the gimbal is able to more freely rotate in the yaw axis as it was intended.
