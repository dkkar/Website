Initial Basketball Tracking Test

Posted on January 23, 2020 by Sanjay Srinivasan

Today we performed initial testing of the basketball tracking scheme:

The intended idea for this portion of the project is to be able to detect the location of a basketball given a video or image feed. Our first test consisted of using an already premade basketball tracking model. However, this proved not to be successful in tracking the basketball when it is not up close. The next step will be to train our own model and retry these tests once more to see if the results improve.
