Power Distribution Board Complete!

Posted on January 20, 2020 by Mauris Winata

After careful consideration and design, the power distribution board that will be incorporated as part of this gimbal design has been fabricated. Here is the completed design:

  • This board has three main purposes:

    • Step down and regulate the battery voltage to 5V
    • Gimbal motor ON/OFF switch
      • Turns off the motors while keeping the main electronics powered for debugging purposes
    • Interface with all electronics featured in the design, namely the controller, IMU and computer vision platform.

The board was sent to the 3D print center in E5 for fabrication and was completed in less than a week. Here is the final result:

We hope to perform testing on this board once all the components arrive in the post. God willing, it will be very soon.
